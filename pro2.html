banana pop pop

I had learned how to make banana pop pop in my shool since last year.so sweet when eating that's why i want to share the recipey with you people.

ingrediant

  • banana
  • flour
  • vanilla flavour
  • oil
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • grapes
  • yiest
  • boil water

steps

  1. bring 3 banana remove the shell and put insite blander then add vanilla flavour,sugar,grape and blend it
  2. took out your boil water and put yiest inside
  3. bring a bowl and put 3 cup of flour then bring your blended banana and pour it inside your flour then add your yiest that is inside boiling water and put 1 tea spoon of baking powder and mixed it very well
  4. took out to the sun for atleast 15 to 20 minute
  5. when you bring it back take a non stict frying pan and pour oil when the oil is hot
